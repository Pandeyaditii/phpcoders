About Us

Situated on a hilltop between beautiful Pine trees, it is just 2 Kms from the main Chandigarh-Shimla Highway with close proximity to Kasauli and Dagshai Cantonments.

Jungle Lodge Resort offers individual rooms, cottages, royal suits, all weather heated pool villas and apartments (each with a private garden/balcony). Come down to enjoy range of amenities like live music /open bar/sports / conferences/marriages/bonfire and many more.

From barbecue in lush green gardens on a hilltop to hosting a jungle retreat, Jungle Lodge Resort offers world-class facilities and services that offer you warmth in stay away from home.
